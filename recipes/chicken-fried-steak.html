Chicken Fried Steak

Description

Chicken Fried Steak is made with cuts of cube steak that have been battered and fried - similar to the way chicken is fried, hence its name. People love this dish because it really sticks to the ribs and can be made a million different ways according to your preference.

Personally, I make my Chicken Fried Steak without the gravy because the dish is already thick enough! But there are plenty of gravy recipes and options out there if you prefer yours with some extra sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cube Steak
  • Panko Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Sour Cream
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Seasonings of your choice

Steps

  1. Season the cube steak to your liking and let sit for an hour. Recommended: Salt, cayenne pepper, herbs, cumin, paprika, ground corriander.
  2. Mix the sour cream and eggs together in a bowl and fully coat each cube steak with the mixture.
  3. On a plate, mix the flour and panko crumbs together with garlic salt and other seasonings to taste.
  4. Lay your coated cube steak on this plate and completely cover the coated steaks with the flour and panko crumb mix.
  5. Fry with vegetable oil on medium heat.
  6. Once crispy on the outside and with the meat cooked through, lay them on a paper towel to disperse some of the oil
  7. Serve with sides of your choice.
